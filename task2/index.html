Noctilucous Jade

Noctilucous Jade is dropped by Noctilucous Jade nodes, which are found at the base of cliffs and in caves and mines throughout Liyue; many can notably be found around Mingyun Village. Like all ores, Noctilucous Jade nodes are best damaged by Blunt Attacks or Geo DMG. Each node can drop one to three Noctilucous Jades when broken.
